About App

Mobile Pharmarcist Assistant

The development of Pharmacist Mobile Assistants was motivated due to the challenges that pharmacists has sometimes in getting information about drugs, at times they may need to peruse a huge study materials or making several calls before achieving their aim which is time delayed, unreliable and affect them in delivering real time medication and advice to the patients. Also, patients may also need at times reaching the pharmacist before getting prescription and dosage information about a drugs.

Aim and Objectives

The aim of this research is to develop Pharmacist mobile Assistant System that provides information such components or composition, manufacturing details and also the dosage about drugs to pharmacist. Objectives of the system include:

  • To develop a system that will give the pharmacist opportunity to access essential drug information using an integrated database system
  • To design a system that will provide timely, effective medication information about drug which is accessible anywhere using android studio.
  • Evaluate the accuracy and acceptability of the system using valid test data
